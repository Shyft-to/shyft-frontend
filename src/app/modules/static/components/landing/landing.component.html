Developing NFT Application Is Time Taking and Expensive

Blockchain stack is still nascent and complex

Launching a scalable NFT app involves multiple moving parts in on-chain (SDKs, IPFS, RPC nodes, smart contracts, wallets) and off-chain (caching, DBs, auto-scaling) logic, with rapidly changing protocols and documentation.

Development cost is high

This usually requires a team of highly skilled and diverse developers, increasing the development cost by a huge margin.

Slow go to Market is the biggest risk

Teams spend a significant amount of time reinventing the wheel (caching, making NFTs queryable, etc.) instead of focusing on product and business logic, increasing the go-to-market time for web3 apps.
